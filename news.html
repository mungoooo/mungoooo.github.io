Chrome wins best Score at POV24!September 29th, 2024

After a busy weekend of film screenings, Mungo McLaggan was delighted to take home the award of best score for his work on the short film Chrome at this year's Persistence of Vision Film Festival.

After a busy weekend of film screenings, Mungo McLaggan was delighted to take home the award of best score for his work on the short film Chrome at this year's Persistence of Vision Film Festival.

Being partially a dance film, it’s no surprise that music forms an integral part of Chrome. Mungo McLaggan’s score spans a wide range of musical genres from bumping dance music to introspective solo piano. The whole soundtrack is out now on all major streaming platforms.

The whole Chrome team was thrilled when, later in the night, the film went on to win both best cinematography and best lead performer!
